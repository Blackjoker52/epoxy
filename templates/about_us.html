About us

Epoxy 214

At Epoxy 214, we specialize in transforming spaces with our top-quality epoxy flooring. Trained under the education and expertise of Epoxy 239, we bring a fresh and reliable approach to every project. Our team, with extensive experience, is dedicated to offering durable and aesthetically pleasing solutions tailored to meet the specific needs of each client.

From garages to terraces and patios, we are committed to providing exceptional service and results that exceed expectations. We believe in combining functionality and design, ensuring that each installation is not only long-lasting but also visually impactful.

Additionally, at Epoxy 214, we offer specialized courses for those interested in learning epoxy application techniques. Our goal is to share our knowledge and passion, ensuring that every project reflects the highest quality and professionalism.
